LASAGNA



Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is both delicious and comforting. It is made with layers of flat, wide pasta noodles, alternating with fillings of meat sauce, béchamel sauce (white sauce), vegetables, and cheese. The lasagna is then baked until the noodles are tender and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

INGREDIENTS

  • noodles
  • meat sauce
  • bechamel sauce
  • vegetables
  • cheese

STEPS:

  1. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce or marinara sauce in a baking dish.
  2. Top with pre-cooked lasagna noodles (oven-ready or briefly boiled).
  3. Add dollops of ricotta cheese or cottage cheese, spreading it partially.
  4. Sprinkle generously with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Bake the lasagna for 20-25 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown on top.
  7. Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
